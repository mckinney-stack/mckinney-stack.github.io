About.


I’m a self-taught front-end developer with a passion for delivering high-quality, user-friendly programmes. My journey began when I started working with music technology in education and subsequently in a professional setting. As an engineer and sales rep in this environment, it was crucial to have a solid understanding of the technology involved, and a capacity for problem-solving was integral.

After working with various music software programmes, I began developing my own using Max/MSP. I then started developing other programmes with JavaScript and I was hooked instantly. I loved the scope and depth of the language and the fact there was so much to learn – it also just felt highly gratifying to play around with a programme of my own design!

I’m a creative and hard-working individual, as exemplified by my first class degree and top college grades – which earned me a discount at university – so programming feels like the natural next-step in my career. I love the fact that I’ll constantly be growing, developing and learning as a programmer – which is one of the primary factors that attracted me to the field.

I’m looking forward to being part of a vibrant team, as I was in my recent sales executive and management role, selling high-tech, specialist music equipment – and consistently ranking as one of the company’s top salesmen and service-providers each month.
